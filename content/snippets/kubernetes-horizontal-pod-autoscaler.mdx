## Overview

Kubernetes Horizontal Pod Autoscaler with multiple scaling metrics (CPU, memory, custom metrics, external metrics) and fine-tuned scaling behavior. Includes stabilization windows to prevent thrashing and asymmetric scale-up/scale-down policies.

## Code

```yaml
behavior:
  scaleUp:
    stabilizationWindowSeconds: 30
    policies:
      - type: Percent
        value: 100
        periodSeconds: 30
  scaleDown:
    stabilizationWindowSeconds: 300
```

## Notes

- `stabilizationWindowSeconds` prevents rapid oscillation by requiring metrics to be sustained
- Scale-up is aggressive (double in 30s) while scale-down is conservative (10% per minute)
- `selectPolicy: Max` for scale-up picks the policy that adds the most pods
- `selectPolicy: Min` for scale-down picks the policy that removes the fewest pods
- Resource `requests` on pods are required for CPU/memory-based HPA to calculate utilization
- Custom metrics require a metrics adapter (Prometheus adapter, Datadog, etc.)
