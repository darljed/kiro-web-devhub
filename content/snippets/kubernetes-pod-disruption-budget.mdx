## Overview

Pod Disruption Budgets protect service availability during voluntary disruptions (node drains, cluster upgrades, autoscaler scale-down). Combined with topology spread constraints, they ensure geographic distribution and minimum replicas during maintenance.

## Code

```yaml
apiVersion: policy/v1
kind: PodDisruptionBudget
spec:
  minAvailable: 2          # OR
  maxUnavailable: 25%      # percentage of total replicas
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: my-service
```

## Notes

- PDBs only affect voluntary disruptions (node drain, autoscaler); not involuntary (OOM, node crash)
- `minAvailable` guarantees a minimum replica count; `maxUnavailable` limits simultaneous disruptions
- Use percentage for services that auto-scale (scales with replica count)
- Use absolute numbers for critical services with fixed replica requirements
- Topology spread constraints distribute pods across failure domains (zones, nodes)
- PDB + anti-affinity ensures a single node failure cannot take the service offline
